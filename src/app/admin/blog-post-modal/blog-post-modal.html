Maintaining healthy teeth is essential for your overall well-being. Here are five expert-recommended tips:

1. **Brush Twice Daily**: Use fluoride toothpaste and brush for at least two minutes each time. Make sure to brush all surfaces of your teeth.

2. **Floss Daily**: Flossing removes plaque and food particles from between teeth where your toothbrush can't reach.

3. **Limit Sugary Foods**: Sugar feeds harmful bacteria in your mouth. Try to limit sugary snacks and drinks.

4. **Visit Your Dentist Regularly**: Schedule checkups every six months for professional cleaning and early detection of issues.

5. **Use Mouthwash**: An antimicrobial mouthwash can help reduce bacteria and freshen your breath.

Following these simple tips can help you maintain a healthy, beautiful smile for years to come!
